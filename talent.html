July 17, 2019 • by Faria Rehman

They helped move the world. Learn how they can help your team.

We all know that the COVID-19 pandemic has had a significant impact on people and businesses around the world, and VROOM is no exception. The world has been on pause for the last few months causing the way we move around our cities to change overnight. As a result, we have had to make the incredibly hard decision to say goodbye to some of our most talented employees to ensure that our business comes out the other side of this crisis stronger.

Following the example of other businesses affected by COVID-19, we have created a public talent directory to profile our impacted employees and help companies who are hiring to easily connect with the talented and passionate people who have helped move the world. The directory shares information about their experience, location and can include links to their social channels and portfolios (where applicable).

Dwell-time dilemma

To put it simply, VROOM employees are dynamic and driven. They pull together and react at lightning speed in an ever-changing environment to take on challenges that no one has encountered before. They build with a resilience that has allowed us to innovate and scale the business. Making a difference in the cities we live in is a fundamental part of our mission and it’s something that our team members constantly work towards. It’s this mentality that truly sets VROOM employees apart and that has grown and scaled our business over the last ten years. Without their contribution, VROOM would not be where it is today.

At the Georgia facility, LG made the following changes to reduce driver wait times and unlock higher facility ratings: Dock Management: Moved drop trailers from outbound dock doors with less capacity and high variability to receiving docks. Staffing: Created a swing shift to minimize the effect of staffing transitions during peak periods in the day. Appointment flexibility: Instituted flexible appointments to decrease dwell time.

As a talent acquisition leader, I would like to call on companies who are looking to hire passionate and dynamic people to move their businesses farther – this talent directory is here to help. Reach out and ask those who are profiled about the things they helped build at VROOM. I’m convinced they can be of great value to your team.
